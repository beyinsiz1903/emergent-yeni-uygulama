### Fire Alarm
1. Evacuate building per protocol
2. Call emergency services
3. Check fire panel location
4. Reset only after clearance

### Water Leak
1. Shut off main water valve (location: _____)
2. Notify front desk (affected rooms)
3. Begin water extraction
4. Document damage with photos

### Power Outage
1. Check circuit breakers
2. Start generator (if equipped)
3. Notify management
4. Call utility company

### Elevator Emergency
1. Calm trapped guests via intercom
2. Call elevator service company
3. Do NOT attempt manual rescue
4. Document incident
